Featured projects

A few representative research and engineering efforts.

Research

Outdoor mmWave channel sounding at 28 GHz

Building a coherent distributed MIMO channel sounder with GPSDO-disciplined timing, IF-over-fiber links, and synchronized transmitter carts for urban outdoor measurements.

Signal processing

Wideband MPC extraction pipeline

Developed a MATLAB pipeline that treats delay-power profiles like images, detects peaks robustly, and clusters multipath components with DBSCAN for correlation analysis.

Communications

OTFS channel estimation and pilot design

Implemented a full OTFS transceiver chain and proposed coded pilot schemes that improved BER and reduced PAPR in NTN-style channels.

Optical systems

Multi-mode fiber channel modeling

Modeled MMF propagation, modal dispersion, and mode-dependent loss, then evaluated MMSE and ZF receiver behavior across channel conditions.
